The Data Challenge

Our raw financial data (2022-2024) had several issues that needed cleaning:

  • Inconsistent formatting: Merged cells, random commas in numbers
  • Embedded tax calculations: Revenue figures mixed with VAT computations
  • Missing department labels: Unclear entries like "ma", "re", "ki"
  • Structural inconsistencies: Different formats across years

Our Data Cleaning Process

1. Standardized Formatting

Removed commas, fixed decimal places, and created consistent column headers across all years.

2. Separated Tax Calculations

Isolated pure revenue/expense values from embedded tax computations to show true business performance.

3. Categorized Departments

Consolidated similar departments (e.g., "Duma" â†’ "DUMA BAR") for accurate year-over-year comparison.

4. Handled Missing Data

Dropped empty rows and filled logical defaults to maintain data integrity.
